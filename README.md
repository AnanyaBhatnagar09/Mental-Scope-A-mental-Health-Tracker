In a digital world, people face mental health problems and often feel helpless as they don’t know what to do and how to cure problems like stress, anxiety, and depression. This is especially common among students and working professionals. Most of the sites are not user interactive and user friendly. We developed a mental health tracker app- Mental Scope, which tracks the person’s mental health using parameters like sleep duration, exercise duration, stress level, fitness level. Based on these parameters, our app produces interactive graphs, through which the user can keep a track on their mental health, by seeing the rise and fluctuations in graph. Mental Scope also has a habit tracker which tracks the user’s habits using some parameters and gives suggestions to users as well. It also has a mapping file, that tells the users  nearby psychiatrists /nearby hospitals if they need support. Mental Scope also has its own inbuilt chatbot Pandora AI integrated with the platform, that is made on Rule based logic and it understands the user’s current state and responds accurately and in depth. Apart from this Mental Scope also has a multi modal face detection app, that detects emotions on the face of the user, it allows users to speak their emotion and considers the voice tone of the user and makes prediction of the emotion. All the entries are saved in a table format along with a time stamp, making it easier and convenient for the user to keep a track of their mental health.
